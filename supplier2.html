Onboarding Steps

Phase 1: Interview

You will complete the following steps to ensure we are the right solution for your company:

-Fill out and submit the New Supplier Form

-Receive an email from a Team Lapalap;
schedule a phone call to review your form.

-Share more specific information about your product offering.

-Move on to the next step if both your company and ours agree this could be a good collaboration.

Phase 2: Documents

At this point, a senior business manager will manage your account. He/She will submit a proposal to you, which will include a request for the following:

-Legal documents such as insurance certificate, of insurance, company MoA, AoA, Memorandum, Incorporation certificate, Pan Card, DIN no. and product guarantee, among others.

-Additional documents specific to your people, products, and consumer, which you will be asked to complete and return.

Phase 3: Data Accuracy

You will work with our master data team to enable Business Associates and consumers to see your products on our e-commerce site and app.

Our team will lead you through item setup and answer any questions you have along the way.

Phase 4: Launch

Once we have reached an agreement and all your product items reflect on website and app, we will establish a launch date and submit our first order.

Help us share the message that your products are now available through Lapalap by using one time complimentary service.

Onboarding Timeline

We believe that the best business partnerships take time and effort. Every step is built to make your journey smoother.

It can take a minimum of 90 days from start to finish. Depends Purely on how quickly you provide the necessary information to us, this timeline can extend three months. Rest assured; its worth the effort. We will increase your reach. Together, we will grow your business.
